34. Donald Arthur HOLMES

Notes from Terry Moulin, 2002:
He was in the Sea Cadets & later the NZ Navy.
Badly injured his leg in a wharf accident while employed at Lyttleton Harbour.
Used to play Rugby league; attended Woolston Primary school.
Was keen on tapestry and did some while in hospital in 1989.
