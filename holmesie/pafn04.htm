Descendants of Robert HOLMES I

Notes

33. Sarah HOLMES

Had 6 children.

34. Dorcas Mary HOLMES

Died unmarried.

35. Charles Davenport HOLMES III

He died unmarried, while a student at Trinity College, Dublin, from the effect of a poisoned wound contracted in the dissecting room. He passed away in 1820.

36. Charles HAMILTON

Emigrated to the USA and was living in Missouri in 1849.
Information from Eric Wray, Nov 2015

22. Thomas HOLMES

Of Glenfin, married Catherine Parker of Glenfin (alive in 1849) by whom he had issue, 1 son and 4 daughters.

52. Dr. Daniel Stewart HOLMES

Studied in Glasgow and Dublin. Obtained his Surgical diploma from the Royal College of Surgeons, London, in 1831. Surgeon to the Poor Law Unions of Colesborne and later of Cirencester, Gloucestershire, England. He passed away without issue in 1845.

54. Mary Elizabeth HOLMES

Mary married John King of Trimera, Letterkenny, by whom she had 2 sons and 4 daughters, one of whom, Miss Katherine Anne King, was still living in 1928 at Portstewart, Londonderry.

Married by the Rev. Robert Holmes.

55. John HOLMES

Divinity student at Glasgow.

56. Rev. Benjamin HOLMES II

Twin brother of Robert Holmes III. Died unmarried.

58. Dr. James HOLMES III

Apprenticed to his brother Charles in Dublin. Qualified as Surgeon at the College of Surgeons, London, in April 1838, after which he was appointed Medical Attendant of the Cirencester Union and resided with his brother, Daniel, until Daniel's death on 23 Jan 1845, when he succeeded to his practice and continued to reside in the same house. Died unmarried.
